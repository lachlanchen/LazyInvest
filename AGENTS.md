# AGENTS.md

## Project Purpose

This repository stores investment research notes and watchlists. Treat all market, company, filing, price, guidance, and news information as date-sensitive.

## Working Rules

- Use current primary sources whenever possible: SEC filings, company investor relations releases, earnings transcripts, exchange data, and reputable market data.
- Include the research date in new notes.
- State clearly when material is a research watchlist rather than personal financial advice.
- Cite source links for company results, guidance, market data, and major industry claims.
- Preserve the distinction between reported GAAP metrics and non-GAAP or adjusted metrics.
- Refresh prices, market caps, guidance, and news before making any current-market claim.
- Keep assumptions visible, especially around growth rates, valuation, catalysts, and risk.

## File Conventions

- Prefer Markdown for research notes.
- Use descriptive filenames with dates, for example `US_Underfollowed_Growth_Stocks_2026-06-13.md`.
- Keep raw downloads, screenshots, and temporary data out of git unless they are intentionally part of the research record.

## Git Notes

- Do not rewrite or delete existing research notes unless explicitly asked.
- Before committing, review `git status` and make sure generated or temporary files are excluded.
